About

Visit our natural paradise and reconnect with yourself

Resting on 30-acres with marvelous views of the ocean and lush scenery, The High Life Villa Resort and Spa is a unique concept for those looking to indulge in the warm sun, gorgeous beaches, and life-affirming cultural encounters of Negril, Jamaica. The High Life is a private, gated, exquisite vacation destination at the West End of Negril, Jamaica- encompassing a 180* view of the beautiful Caribbean Sea, and the sound of the crashing of the waves is Divine. The cool relaxing pool, the tropical sun and breeze command quiet relaxation. The property is planted with Mangoes, Avocado, nees berry, sweet sap, bananas, oranges, lemons and a beautiful array of surreal tropical flowers with year-round blooms.

The detail on the property from the arches to the Mermaids on the bottom of the pool to the tropical landscape makes it a fairytale experience. Perfect for weddings, special occasions, meetings, and events, or just totally checking out from the daily grind. This is a re-energize opportunity. Snorkeling, scuba diving, cliff diving, river tours, glass bottom boats, cave exploration, rain forest exploration, private beach combing, spa, restaurants & bars are all available- there is truly no need to leave this fairytale estate once you arrive.
